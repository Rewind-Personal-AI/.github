## Common Issues and Solutions

- **High CPU usage:** The rewind mac app indexes new content in real time. If CPU usage is consistently high, reduce the video quality setting or exclude resource-intensive apps from recording.
- **Search not finding recent content:** The rewind ai search indexes with a slight delay. Content from the last few minutes may not be immediately searchable—wait 5-10 minutes for recent activity to process.
- **Microphone not recording:** Check that the rewind app has microphone permissions in System Preferences > Privacy & Security > Microphone, and ensure the correct input device is selected in Rewind's audio settings.
- **Storage filling up quickly:** Adjust retention settings to automatically delete recordings older than a specific timeframe, or exclude low-value apps like music players from capture by the rewind for mac.
- **App crashes on launch:** Restart your Mac and ensure you're running the latest version of the rewind software. If problems persist, check Console.app for error logs and contact support.
- **Poor OCR accuracy:** The rewind search tool's text recognition works best with clear, high-resolution text. Low-resolution screens or small fonts may reduce accuracy—try increasing display resolution.
- **Missing screen recordings:** Verify that Screen Recording permission is enabled in System Preferences and that you haven't accidentally added important apps to the exclusion list in the rewind desktop app settings.
